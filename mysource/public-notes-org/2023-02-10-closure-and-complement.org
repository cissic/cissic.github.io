For the given subset $A \in \Omega$

Let $A = <0,1)$, and $\Omega = (-\infty, \infty)$

** Complement:
[[https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Complement_(set_theory)][Complement]]

$A' = \Omega - A$

$A' = (-\infty, 0) \cup <1, \infty)$

** Closure
[[https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Closure_(topology)][Closure]]

$\overline{A} = <0,1>$
