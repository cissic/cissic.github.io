# How to play dvd movie in debian

According to
<https://askubuntu.com/a/887260>

    sudo apt-get install libdvd-pkg
    
    sudo dpkg-reconfigure libdvd-pkg

Then just drag-and-drop the IFO (usually VIDEO<sub>TS.IFO</sub>) into VLC window.
